Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. here is a recipe for deliciously rice scrambled eff

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium head
  2. Gentlu mic the eggs and cream in a bow
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centr every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are moist remove from the heat
